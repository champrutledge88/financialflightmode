Privacy Policy

Last updated July 17, 2026

Information We Collect

The scorecard calculator runs in your browser. The numbers you enter into the scorecard are used to calculate your score on the page and are not stored by this website.

If you request the Starter Kit or Flight Briefing, we collect the email address you submit so we can send the requested resource and related Financial Flight Mode updates.

How We Use Information

We use submitted contact information to deliver the Starter Kit, send Financial Flight Mode emails, and improve the experience for visitors.

We do not sell your personal information.

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors find and use the website, including page visits and privacy-safe funnel events such as starting or completing the Scorecard, viewing a Flight Briefing, completing a signup, and downloading the Starter Kit.

Financial Flight Mode does not send the financial numbers entered into the Scorecard or your email address to Google Analytics. Analytics may process device, browser, approximate location, referral, campaign, and interaction information and may use cookies or similar technologies according to Google's privacy practices.

Email Provider

Email signup information is processed through MailerLite. MailerLite may process data according to its own privacy and security practices.

Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from Financial Flight Mode emails using the unsubscribe link in any email. You can also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your email signup information.

Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to request access to the personal information connected to your email signup, ask for corrections, request deletion, or opt out of certain uses. Financial Flight Mode will respond to valid privacy requests as required by applicable law.

Education Only

Financial Flight Mode provides financial education and planning support. It does not provide personal financial, investment, tax, legal, or debt advice.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact Financial Flight Mode through the email reply path provided in Financial Flight Mode messages.
